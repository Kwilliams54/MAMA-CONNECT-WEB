The Challenge Mothers Face

  • Missed antenatal check-ins delay detection of risks.
  • No continuous monitoring means complications go unnoticed.
  • Clinics lack visibility to prioritize high-risk pregnancies.
  • Rural mothers are excluded from smartphone-only solutions.

Our Solution

Wearable Patch

Tracks baby’s movement and mother’s vitals in real time.

App + SMS/USSD

Delivers alerts, reminders, and education to all mothers, even with basic phones.

Clinic Dashboard

Helps midwives and hospitals prioritize high-risk pregnancies quickly.

Why Mama Connect is Different

Unlike existing solutions, Mama Connect combines continuous monitoring with inclusive access. It works for both smartphone and basic phone users, ensuring no mother is left behind.
